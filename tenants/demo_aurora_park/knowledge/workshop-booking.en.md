To book a workshop: reserve online on our website or sign up at the 3F counter. We suggest booking 3 days ahead, as popular slots fill up. You can cancel or reschedule for free up to 24 hours before; cancellations within 24 hours are non-refundable. Groups of 6+ can call to arrange a private session.
