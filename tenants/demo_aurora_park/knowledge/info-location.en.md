Getting to Aurora Park: we're at No. 88, Guangyun Rd, Xinyi District, Taipei. By MRT, it's about a 5-minute walk from City Hall Station Exit 2; by bus, get off at "Xinyi Civic Center"; if driving, the park has an underground car park (B1–B3).
