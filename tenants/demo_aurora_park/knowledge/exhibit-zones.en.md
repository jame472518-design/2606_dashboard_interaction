Suggested route: the park has 3 floors. Start on 2F with "Dwelling in Light," then come down to 1F for Yun Select shopping, and finish with a break at Aurora Cafe. 3F holds the workshops and Aurora Spa (by appointment). The exhibition has three zones — "Morning Mist," "Flowing Light" and "Dwelling" — about a 40-minute unhurried walk.
