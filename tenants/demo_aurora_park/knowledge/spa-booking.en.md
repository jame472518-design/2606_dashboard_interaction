The 3F "Aurora Spa" offers neck-and-shoulder relief and essential-oil aromatherapy, all by appointment. Book online or by phone, ideally 1–2 days ahead; walk-ins are welcome if there's an open slot. Please arrive 10 minutes early, and let your therapist know in advance if you're pregnant or have any special conditions.
