Any latte at Aurora Cafe can be made with oat or soy milk (+NT$10). The light-meal menu has 2–3 vegetarian/vegan options, marked on the menu. Items containing nuts, dairy, eggs or gluten are labeled; if you have an allergy, just ask the counter and we'll confirm for you.
