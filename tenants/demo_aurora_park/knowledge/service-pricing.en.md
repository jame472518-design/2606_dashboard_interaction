Experience pricing: workshops are NT$800–1,500 per session (by type, materials included). Aurora Spa is NT$1,800 for 60-min neck-and-shoulder relief and NT$2,500 for 90-min aromatherapy. There are often bundle deals — ask the counter when booking. We accept cash, credit cards and mobile payment.
