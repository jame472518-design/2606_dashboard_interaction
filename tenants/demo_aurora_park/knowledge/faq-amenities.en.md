The park has free WiFi named "Aurora-Guest," no password. There are restrooms on every floor. We accept cash, credit cards and mobile payment (LINE Pay, Apple Pay). 3F has an accessible elevator and restroom, strollers are welcome throughout, and the 1F front desk lends wheelchairs.
