Aurora Park is open daily 11:00–21:00. The cafe opens earlier at 10:00; the select-goods shop runs 11:00–21:00; the "Light Box" exhibition on 2F is 11:00–20:00 with last entry at 19:30; the 3F workshops and Aurora Spa are by appointment. Closed on the first Monday of each month.
