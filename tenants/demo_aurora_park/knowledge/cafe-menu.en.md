Aurora Cafe is on 1F. Coffee and drinks run about NT$120–180, including pour-over single origins, lattes, seasonal specials and decaf options; light meals are around NT$150–280, with paninis, salads and a dessert of the day. Seating is first-come; expect a short wait on busy weekends.
